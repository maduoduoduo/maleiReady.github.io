About me

I am an Ph.D. student of computer graphics at State Key Laboratory of Computer Science, Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Yanyun Chen. Before re-engaging in research, I was employed by Autodesk as a graphic engineer for almost 3 years, reported to Zhenggang Yuan. Before that, I studied for my M.Sc. degree at Digital ART(Augmented Reality Tech) Laboratory of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Yang Xubo and Xiao Shuangjiu. I received my B.Eng. degree from Zhejiang University in 2007. I was a visiting researcher in Prof. Hanqiu Sun's group at The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2016. I also studied in Singapore under a exchange program in 2007, supervised by Robert H. Deng.

I successfully defended my PhD dissertation on Nov 22nd, 2018! My committee consisted of Prof. Enhua Wu (UMAC/ZUMR/IS,CAS), Prof. Hua Li (ICT,CAS), Prof. Bao-Gang Hu (IA,CAS), Prof. Xiaopeng Zhang (IA,CAS), Prof. Guangzheng Fei (CUC). A big thank you on Thanksgiving Day, 2018.
